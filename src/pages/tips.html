Recycling Tips

General Recycling Tips

  • Rinse containers before recycling
  • Remove caps and lids from bottles
  • Flatten cardboard boxes
  • Keep plastic bags out of recycling bins
  • Check for recycling symbols on items

Material Guide

Glass

  • Remove caps and corks
  • Rinse thoroughly
  • Sort by color if required

Paper

  • Keep dry and clean
  • Remove plastic wrapping
  • Avoid greasy or food-stained paper

Plastic

  • Check recycling numbers (1-7)
  • Clean from food residue
  • Remove labels when possible

Metal

  • Rinse cans thoroughly
  • Crush to save space
  • Remove paper labels

Common Mistakes

❌ Plastic Bags

Don't put plastic bags in recycling bins - take them to specific collection points.

❌ Greasy Containers

Food-contaminated containers can't be recycled - clean them thoroughly first.

❌ Mixed Materials

Separate different materials before recycling (e.g., paper from plastic windows).
